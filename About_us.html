Three months later, it acquired Italian restaurant search service Cibando.[34] Zomato also acquired Seattle-based food portal, Urbanspoon, for an estimated $60 million in 2015.[35] Other acquisitions of 2015 include Mekanist in an all-cash deal,[36] the Delhi-based startup MapleGraph that built MaplePOS (renamed Zomato Base),[37] and NexTable, a US-based table reservation and restaurant management platform.[38] In 2016, the company acquired Sparse Labs, a logistics technology startup,[39] and the food delivery startup, Runnr, in 2017.[40] In September 2018, Zomato acquired Bengaluru-based food e-marketplace, TongueStun Food, for about $18 million in a cash and stock deal.[41] In December 2018, Zomato acquired Lucknow-based startup, TechEagle Innovations, that works exclusively on drones, for an undisclosed amount.[42] Zomato claimed that the acquisition will help pave the way towards drone-based food delivery in India, building technology aimed at a hub-to-hub delivery network.[43] On 21st January, 2020, Zomato acquired its rival Uber Eats' business in India in an all stock deal.[44][45]
